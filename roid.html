INTRODUCTION

ASTEROIDS - the seperation - the mark. It marks between the hard planet and the gas one, seperating the hot-headed and the level-headed. It reconciles them all.


Like Asteroid, its weilder of personality is shy and bashful. They are forgetful and easily forgetable by others. Yet, without them, no one is safe, no one is protected..

QUIRKS

Understanding: To be able to stand in between the cold and the hot, Aesteroid need to be calm and understanding. So that in times, they can act as judge to make a consensus between two parties.


Hidden potential: Aesteroids are strong and powerful in their own way. They seem and look small, but one asteroid can destroy entirely the surface of one planet.


Tender heart: A sweetheart in a bittersweet world. Don't let it become twisted.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Bashfulness: They are vulnerable. Since asteroids are small and fragile, they tend to be bullied or threatened by others.


Ill-minded: Needs to be protected. Asteroids are afraid of standing or being alone. Most of the time, Jupiter is there for them. But, it's time for Asteroid to find their own place, their own league.


Inferior complexity: As being constantly compared to other planets, aesteroids will underestimate themselves and consider them to be weaklings. This is proved the opposite. Since we all start from being a smal asteroid.
