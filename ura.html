INTRODUCTION

Uranus - the planet of deep sky. It's like the twin with Neptune, both deep and wide. However, if Neptune represents one's artistic side, then Uranus stands for its academic knowledge.


Like Uranus, its weilder of personality is aloof like a cloud. Never really care about anything in this world, but still get to know about everything in existence.

QUIRKS

Excellent listener: Always listen to your story, always give you the soundest advice. Uranus is your mentor, the mentor that you don't think you need, but you have it eventually.


Staying out of trouble: Trouble is troublesome. They are tired of all turmoil in the world already, and all they want is peace. That's why, some of them look up to the cloud. So much.


A friend in need is a friend indeed: They can be careless, but they are there.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Love: Do love exist to Uranus? It's questionable, because their heart is made of cloud. And their soul is made of star. Loving an Uranus, prepared to be lonely for a long night..

A little touch of dumbness: Uranus is a fool when it comes to emotional barriers. Seeking help! Sometimes, matters can only be solved spiritually.
