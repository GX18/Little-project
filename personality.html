INTRODUCTION

Mars - the planet of fire and passsion, captures the heart of his guests with its beauty and mystery at their first glance. Being the closest planet to Earth, Mars is the friendly-planet that you can hear in bedtime story, about the aliens, the rivers, the mountains and the never-ending myths that are related to it


Like Mars, its weilder of personality is as cheerful as a firework, and as amiable as your childhood friends. They are kind-hearted at heart, goofy and open-minded to new things. The mastermind of flexibility and dexterity.

QUIRKS

Open-minded: Susceptible to new things and adventure. They try their best to be understanding, and be supportive of their friends' decisions. Yet, if needed, a Mars will never turn down an offer to bash at you for your stupid choice.


Warrior: Kind-hearted as they are, Mars will never be afraid to take on a one and one battle if their loved is endangered. Don't trigger their hidden selves. Fierce and passionate. It's welcoming to remember that many of Mars can be soldiers.


Fervent lover: Mars are ideal lover. They are devoted, dedicated, and cold-headed at the same time.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Vulenerable: Mars tend to trust. They tend to rely so much on a relationship, and become blind on the way. This may get worse if they are betrayed. Mars' heart will be broken.


Too optimistic: Too much optimism can be a problem sometimes, especially if Mars are in dangerous situations.


Insecurity: Despite shining like a brightful star, Mars are often insecure and complex. They tend to overthink and take words seriously.
