INTRODUCTION

VENUS - the planet of love and passion - the capturor of heart and soul. Indulging in Venus is indulging in desire and lust, in everlasting flame of alluration.


Like Venus, its weilder of personality is a social butterfly. They are cheerful, socially-gifted, magnificient and eloquent. They are the ambassador that we need and have.

QUIRKS

Passionate: To Venus, love is unarguably the most integral part of life. They are fervent lovers that dominate their heart, that devote all their time and effort to achieve their goal.


Beauty: Venus is beautiful. Not only physically but also mentally. And they think themselves beautiful. They love themselves, and that's a good thing! They nourish their characteristics and dreams to sow the seeds in the future.


An excellent speakers: Eloquent and flexible. Venus is excellent guest speaker that can deliver their voice just like what Saturns can. However, opposite to Saturn's focus on mind, Venus focuses on emotion, to evict the underlying love within them.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Too talkative: Sometimes, listening is the best method. Just sit down and lend an ear to other stories. You will learn a lot of things from them!


Not recipocated: Venus desires love. Love is so important to them that if their love is not returned, they will become depressed.


Overwhelming: Because Venus loves so much, their love is like fire. It burns, it hurts, it gives you no freedom. It's worthwhile to remember that sometimes, lettting it go is a love choice also.
