INTRODUCTION

Saturn - the combination of death and live - the mark between hell and heaven, between isolation and rejoice.


Like Saturn, its weilder of personality is unpredictable. They can be a sweetheart, they can be a cinnamon roll. They can be a power hungry controller, or a manipulative politician. Yet, in the end, they are themselves.

QUIRKS

Versatile: Saturners can be anything. They can be sweet, they can be strict. Maybe that's just a mask to protect them and to adapt to the environment, after all, it is the second biggest planet, so other have to look up to it.


The mastermind: In the battlefield, you have the warriors and the Saturners. Unlike Mars, they use their brain to fight. Unlike Mercury, they use their voice as weapons. They are great public speakers who shout out their opinions and enable others to follow them.


Patient: Despite the turbulence that they cause, Saturn is always calm and collected. They smile all the time, even in dire situations. That make them the most unpredicatble planet in the solar system.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Too manipulative: People will untrust them. Sometimes, Saturn makes use of others, and hurts them undesirably. It's necessary to take note that, power can be returned in a different ways..


Too ambitious: The higher you get, the lower chances you have to get down. Don't climb too high on the tree, just high enough to make you free.


Trust-issue: It's like a cycle. You distrust others, others distrust you. Saturns put on such a facade, because in the beginning, Saturn has to fight over the shadow the Jupiter gives him.
