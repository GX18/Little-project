QUIRKS

Multi-talented: As Neptune, Neptuner tends to be multi-tasked. They excel at many things in one, and polish each tasks perfectly. In short, they are perfectionist! Leaving matters at their hands and you will never be worried.


An Artist: Beautiful as its mother planet, Neptuners bear an undying love for arts. No matter which form, arts will always have its soft spots in Neptune's profession. Their talent, together with their creativity, created masterpieces.


The reconcilation: Having a war? Neptune is there. Like Earth, they dislike peace. They want justice and rights to be done right. However, Neptuners are not risk-takers. They approach matters in a careful demeanour, enabling the 'victims' to get accustomed to them first.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Over-critical: Being too perfect might pose a problem to Neptune. They will overcritize or overestimate a problem, creating turbulence among their friendship and lovelife. Expect things to have its flaws, drawbacks and downfalls!


Strenous: Don't put such a high standard on your self. People expect you to be flawed, not flawless, and flaws also make one distinguished.


Dreams: Don't be afraid and follow your dream! Neptune are realistic. They think with their mind not their heart, so sometimes decisions are made with regrets. Be more willing to take risks. Risks make success.
