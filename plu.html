INTRODUCTION

Pluto - the lonely planet - the smallest of its all, the forgotten. Hid in the shadow of its brothers and sisters, Pluto sits there, striving to have its own glory.


Like Pluto, its weilder of personality is timid and introverted. They are the black sheep of the family, the one who is left behind. However, they still grow strong, vow to become one of the most mysterious planet in the solar system.

QUIRKS

Level-headed: Calm, collected and silent. Plutos are the quiet type in class or in family. They hid in the shadow like a prey, patiently controls its fates.


Observant: No turmoil are raised in Pluto's dominance. They stay back and stand behind them all, look at other planets and study the features. May that grant them the holy knowledge of acknowledgement.


Mature: Like a lone wolf, they learn to cope with problems alone. They are seperated, after all. Pluto thinks ahead of age and tends to look old, making them a reliable person to lean on in dire needs.

Weakenesses and Overcoming

Egoistic: The coldness might be a facade, but people think the opposite. Some might accuse Pluto of being ignorant and cold-hearted, and give them the cold shoulder. This breaks their tender soul, indeed.


Loneliness: Being isolated, Pluto suffers from loneliness. They are overshadowed and become only a side characters in other stories who never get a happy ending. Be more cheerful and try to open or reach other helpful hands.


Rejection: So what's left? Isolation? Darkness? Or just a simple rejection of every choice, every courage, every love that they bear. That's a nail in the coffin for Pluto. Be kind to them. Those legitimate and small child of Solar System.
